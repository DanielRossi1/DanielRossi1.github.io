I am a final-year PhD Candidate in Artificial Intelligence at the [University of Modena and Reggio Emilia](https://www.ingmo.unimore.it/it), working at [AImageLab](https://aimagelab.ing.unimore.it/) under the supervision of Prof. Roberto Vezzani.

My research focuses on **Efficient Deep Learning** and **Edge AI deployment**. I specialize in designing ultra-lightweight neural networks for computer vision tasks, with applications in **surveillance**, **disaster monitoring**, and **monocular depth estimation**.


Previously, I received my Master's Degree _cum laude_ with a thesis entitled "3D Human pose estimation from RGB images with Transformers and Deformable Attention". During this period, I also had the opportunity to work for 6 months as an intern at [Toyota Motor Europe](https://www.toyota-europe.com/) in Brussels, focusing on NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, 3D Pose and Hand Estimation.
